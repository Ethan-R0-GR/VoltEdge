Our Services

We offer a wide range of electrical, solar, and aquatic pump services for homes and businesses across Cape Town. All work is carried out by certified technicians and meets the required South African standards.

Electrical Services

Our general electrical services cover everything from small repairs to full commercial installations. All work is done in line with SANS 10142 standards and our electricians are registered with the Department of Labour.

Residential Electrical

We handle all home electrical needs including the following.

  • Geyser installations and replacements
  • Plug point and light switch installations
  • Prepaid electricity meter installations
  • Inverter and UPS installations for load shedding
  • Outdoor and security lighting

Commercial Electrical

We work with small and medium businesses to keep their electrical systems safe and compliant.

  • Three-phase power installations
  • Industrial machine wiring
  • Emergency lighting systems
  • Electrical compliance audits
  • Scheduled maintenance plans

Certificates of Compliance

A Certificate of Compliance is legally required when selling a property in South Africa. Our registered electricians carry out the full inspection and issue the COC documentation within 24 to 48 hours.

Distribution Board Upgrades

Old or overloaded distribution boards are a safety risk. We replace and upgrade boards to handle modern electrical loads safely and correctly.

Solar and Renewable Energy

With load shedding and rising electricity costs, solar energy is one of the best investments a homeowner or business can make. Our certified solar specialists design and install systems that match your energy needs and your budget.

Solar Panel Installation

We supply and install grid-tied, off-grid, and hybrid solar systems.

  • On-site energy assessment and system sizing
  • Supply and installation of solar panels and inverter
  • Municipal grid application assistance where applicable
  • System monitoring setup

Battery Storage and Hybrid Systems

Hybrid systems store excess solar energy in batteries so you can use it at night or during load shedding. We supply and install lithium-ion battery systems and hybrid inverters.

Solar Geyser Installation and Maintenance

A solar geyser can reduce your water heating costs by up to 40 percent. We install and service both flat plate and evacuated tube solar geyser systems.

  • New solar geyser installations
  • Retrofit onto existing geyser systems
  • Annual service and pressure valve checks
  • Fault diagnosis and element replacements

Pool, Hot Tub, and Water Feature Pumps

VoltEdge Solutions is one of the few companies in Cape Town to specialise in aquatic pump systems alongside electrical and solar work. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all major brands of pool, spa, and water feature pumps.

Pool Pump Servicing and Repair

A faulty pool pump leads to poor filtration and costly chemical problems. We carry out full diagnostic checks and get most pumps repaired on the same day.

  • Motor winding testing and replacement
  • Impeller and seal replacement
  • Basket and filter inspection
  • New pump supply and installation for all major brands
  • Variable speed pump upgrades to save electricity

Hot Tub and Spa Pump Repair

Spa and hot tub systems are complex and need specialist knowledge. Our technicians work with all major spa brands and can source replacement parts quickly.

  • Jet pump diagnosis and motor replacement
  • Circulation pump servicing
  • Wet end seal and gasket replacement
  • Control board and wiring fault diagnosis
  • Full spa electrical safety inspection

Water Feature and Fountain Pump Maintenance

We service and repair submersible and external water feature pumps of all sizes.

  • Pump cleaning and descaling
  • Submersible pump replacement
  • Flow rate adjustment
  • Electrical wiring inspection and repair
  • Timer and float switch installations
